Server Rules

Where can I read the rules?

Go to the Asterisk Rules section and click RP Rules or Robbery Rules. Then choose English or Sinhala.

Do I need to read rules before whitelist?

Yes. You must read and understand the server rules before submitting your whitelist application.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for whitelist?

How long does approval take?

Usually staff review applications as soon as possible.

What are the requirements?
